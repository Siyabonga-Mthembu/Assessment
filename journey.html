It is important to remember that life is a long journey and people have a different pace, so it's not a one-size-fits-all situation.

At that period of time we were a big family made up of Sisters,brother, male and female cousins, and our parents who were helping us with a patterns of directions and parthways which we systemetically call it a life journey today.
